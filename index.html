Have a say

Many traditional charities are a black box. You give money but don't know where it goes. With Giveth, you can choose which specific milestones your donations are used for.

Results matter

We built the Giveth network on community values of transparancy and accountability. Unlike traditional donating, milestones must be completed before donations are released. Milestone completions are confirmed by a simple review process.
